Top foods that you can eat in Kashmir:

  1. Rogan Josh:
    • A flavorful and aromatic slow-cooked curry made with tender pieces of meat, usually lamb or goat. The rich blend of spices, including saffron, gives it a distinctive taste.
  2. Yakhni:
    • A light and nutritious broth typically made with yogurt, meat (often chicken or mutton), and a variety of aromatic spices. Yakhni is a soothing dish, perfect for colder weather.
  3. Wazwan Cuisine:
    • A grand feast comprising multiple dishes, Wazwan is a significant part of Kashmiri culture. It includes delicacies like Rista (meatballs in a spicy red curry), Tabak Maaz (fried ribs), and Dum Aloo (potatoes in a flavorful yogurt-based gravy).
  4. Kahwa:
    • A traditional Kashmiri green tea infused with aromatic spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. Almonds and saffron are often added, creating a fragrant and refreshing beverage.
  5. Kashmiri Pulao:
    • A fragrant rice dish cooked with a mix of dry fruits, saffron, and spices. The mild and sweet flavors make it a delightful accompaniment to various Kashmiri main dishes.

Exploring Kashmir's cuisine provides a unique culinary experience,
with a perfect blend of aromatic spices and local flavors.
